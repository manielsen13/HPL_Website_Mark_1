ABOUT US
Hitting Performance Lab is a baseball facility that specializes in training hitters. HPL was created with the goal of providing the highest quality information, coaching, and resources to each athlete who comes through the doors. We believe in a holistic approach to hitting development, and our process can be adapted and applied to hitters of all ages.
We believe there are 4 Pillars of Hitting: Confidence, Approach, Physical Strength, and Mechanics.
At HPL, we work on improving each aspect of a hitter. Pitchers don't just practice throwing fastballs. Infielders don't just practice fielding regular ground balls. In order to be a great hitter, you need to work on more than just the swing.
TECHNOLOGY
Along with our years of playing and coaching experience, we use the latest hitting development technology to give athletes actionable feedback. We use tools like the Blast Motion sensor, Rapsodo, Gamesense, and Spinball to capture data for hitting metrics. Using these to supplement training helps athletes and coaches better understand problems so they can collaborate to find creative solutions.
